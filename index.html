About

I’m a Design System Specialist at Absa, with 3.5 years of experience in the UX design industry. The name, Amentor, is a mix of Amisha and mentor, I created this space to support designers and help them reach new potentials.

What I offer

1:1 Mentorship

We will set an action plan for your growth areas and have biweekly sessions for 6 months.

Porfolio Reviews

A detailed review of your design portfolio, including how you can improve on design and layout.

UI/UX Feedback

General feedback on design systems, accessibility, usability and design best practices.

Get in touch with me

Send an email with the following:

  • A short message about what kind of mentorship you are looking for;
  • or, if you're looking for design feedback, send me your portfolio, CV, and LinkedIn profile.

If you are looking for mentorship, expect a reply to your email within 2 days to schedule a coffee chat. If you're requesting feedback only, you'll receive it within 2 weeks.
